# IFSTA Suite E7 Highlight

## Goal

Present Essentials 7th Edition as the flagship highlight within IFSTA Suite instead of as a separate portfolio app.

## Design

- Remove the standalone `ifsta-e7` object from `APPS`.
- Keep the combined entry named `IFSTA Suite` with its `13+` icon, role, suite-focused stack, and suite metrics.
- Update the IFSTA Suite description to identify Essentials 7th Edition as the flagship app and summarize its firefighter-training content.
- Add `100K+ Essentials 7th Ed downloads` as the leading IFSTA Suite metric, followed by the existing suite metrics.
- Do not change rendering logic, markup, CSS, or interactions.

## Verification

The drawer contains four apps, has no standalone Essentials 7th Ed icon, and the IFSTA Suite detail includes the E7 highlight plus `100K+`, `70%`, and `13+` metrics.
